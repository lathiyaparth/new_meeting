How to Join Meeting(with LL):-

LL join meeting is very simple. LL is provide few step for join meeting.

  • step 1: select meeting option (icon)
  • step 2: enter your Name.
  • step 3: after join a meeting button click and start

After you access the meeting and use start video, microphone, chatting, and more.




How to Create Meeting(with member):-

LL Create meeting is very simple. LL is provide few step for join meeting.

  • step 1: select meeting option (icon) and select connect with meeting for member
  • step 2: enter your Name and ID(not meeting Id).
  • step 3: after join a meeting button click and start
  • step 4: top-right icon with join meeting

After you access the meeting and use start video, microphone, chatting, and more.
